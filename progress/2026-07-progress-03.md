## 1) Summary
During this reporting period, NurseLink continued operating its B2C home nursing service while developing Meridian, its B2B care operations platform.

The B2C service provides the team with real operational experience in nurse onboarding, patient intake, matching, scheduling, visit coordination, payments and care documentation. These workflows are being used to improve Meridian for healthcare, community-care and assisted-living organisations.

## 2) Objectives recap
- Scale B2C adoption with stronger fulfilment and lower-friction intake.
- Build a configurable B2B care operations platform (Meridian) for home care, assisted living, community care and hospital-at-home workflows.
- Improve trust, quality assurance, nurse verification, escalation processes and compliance readiness.
- Convert early conversations into pilots, recurring care arrangements or paying B2B users.

## 3) B2C home nursing progress
- NurseLink has onboarded more than 80 nurses.
- The platform has supported or engaged more than 60 patients and families.
- Supported more than 100 hours of home nursing bookings.
- The team continued handling requests involving routine home support, recurring care, overnight supervision and clinical nursing procedures.
- Service categories and pricing were simplified to make it clearer which requests require routine support, recurring clinical support, higher-support continuity or procedure-based nursing.
- Nurse verification processes were strengthened. The current workflow includes checking nurses through the Singapore Nursing Board public register, recording Practising Certificate status and retaining declarations regarding clinical experience and BCLS, CPR or AED qualifications.
- B2C operations continue to provide feedback for improving intake, matching, scheduling, documentation and payment workflows.

## 4) Meridian B2B platform development
Meridian is now positioned as a configurable care operations platform rather than a platform built only for one organisation.

Current or planned capabilities include:
- Patient or resident intake
- Patient and resident records
- Nurse and care worker profiles
- Skill and service matching
- Scheduling and task allocation
- Routing
- Live visit status
- Vitals and care documentation
- Incident and follow-up tracking
- Operational reporting
- Organisation accounts
- Medication management

The medication management workflow is currently being developed based on feedback from assisted-living operators.

The initial pilot pricing is S$99 per month with no long-term commitment. This allows organisations to test whether the platform reduces manual coordination, improves documentation completeness and makes resident or patient information easier to track.

## 5) B2B discussions and deployments

- Joo Chiat Social Club is assessing Meridian for its assisted-living operations at Camino House.

### Queen's Life Guards Corp and SparkX+Change
- NurseLink is participating in the SparkX+Change 2026 programme under Alexandra Hospital.
- The team is working with Queen's Life Guards Corp on a possible senior health monitoring initiative.
- NurseLink may provide the technology platform for collecting, organising and reviewing vital-sign data.
- The intended outcome is to help identify changes in seniors' health earlier and support efforts to reduce avoidable hospital readmissions.
- The project remains at the problem discovery and programme development stage.

### Industry discussions
- The team held early industry discussions with care-service operators about whether Meridian could complement existing service workflows.
- These conversations remain preliminary and should not be described as confirmed partnerships.

### Additional community-care exploration
- The team explored whether Meridian could support additional community-care use cases beyond its current assisted-living and home-care focus.
- Some opportunities were assessed as less suitable because Meridian's current workflows did not closely match the partner's service vertical.
- The team decided to focus resources on organisations with a stronger operational fit.

### NUHS@Home

- No active pilot with NUHS@Home has started.
- NurseLink has broadened Meridian beyond hospital-at-home use cases to community care, private home care and assisted living.
